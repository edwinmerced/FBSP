Competency 1: Communication Skills

Learning Module 1.5: Groups, communication, and differences


How do you participate in group discussion? How do you communicate effectively with diverse populations? How can you develop respect for the opinions, customs, and individual differences of others
Photo Credit: iStockPhoto/diatrezor


Learn


Module Introduction. In this module you will explore group discussion participation. You will also examine how to communicate effectively with diverse populations. Finally, you will learn about respect for individual differences, opinions and customs.
Learning Targets. Upon the completion of this module you will be able to:

  • Participate in a group discussion as a member and as a leader
  • Demonstrate ability to communicate effectively with diverse populations
  • Demonstrate respect for the opinions, customs, and individual differences of others

Assess


Module Summary: In this module you explored group discussion participation. You also examined how to communicate effectively with diverse populations. Finally, you learned about respect for individual differences of others and their opinions and customs.

Module Exit Assessment 1.5

This assessment will consist of an assignment submission.] You will have to demonstrate your understanding of groups, communication, and differences.

General instructions to access the assessment

To complete this assessment:

  1. Review the module learning materials and perform the practice exercise prior to taking this assessment.
  2. Prepare, review and save a document that will address the two parts of this assignment.
    • Part A: Write a two-page letter describing either the best or the worst meeting you have attended. Discuss why it was either successful or a failure.
    • Part B: Write a two-page paper discussing the diversity you have encountered in your job. How has it changed your attitudes toward fellow employees and guests?
    • Press the "Go to Exit Assessment" button.
    • Follow the instructions to complete your module exit assessment.
