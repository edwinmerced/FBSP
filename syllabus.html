Program Introduction


Introduction to Program. This Food and Beverage Specialist Program is designed to enhance competencies and skills for a career in the hospitality industry. The program will cover content related to the food and beverage industry while covering concepts in relation to menu planning, purchasing, costs, storage, beverage management and industry trends. This certificate program will provide you with industry recognized academic credentials.
Competencies. Upon the completion of this program you will be able to master competencies in the following areas:

  • Communications Skills
  • Professionalism
  • Time Management Skills
  • Knowledge Acquisition Skills
  • Customer Service Skills
  • Technology Skills
