Module Exit Assessment 1.7

This assessment will consist of a assigment submission. You will have to demonstrate your understanding of effective oral presentations.

General instructions to access the assessment

To complete this assessment:

  1. Review the module learning materials and perform the practice exercise prior to taking this assessment.
  2. Download and review the "Food and Beverage Specialist Communication Individual Video Presentation Student Guideline"
  3. Download and review the "Food and Beverage Specialist Communication Individual Video Presentation Student Rubric"
  4. Prepare a 10-minute oral presentation, using the presentation guideline and the rubric. Submit your presentation.
  5. Press the "Go to Exit Assessment" button.
  6. Follow the instructions to complete your module exit assessment.
